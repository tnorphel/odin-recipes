Skyu

Description

Skyu (pronounced as skew) is kind of like a Ladakhi pasta.

Ingredients

  • Wholemeal wheat flour (advisable not to use refined flour)
  • Potatoes
  • Green peas
  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Coriander
  • Salt
  • Turmeric powder
  • Cumin powder
  • Red Chilli powder
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Knead the dough. Do not add anything before kneading. Also, the dough should be relatively dry.Set it aside.
  2. Heat a few teaspoons of cooking oil in a deep pan over low to medium heat.
  3. Add chopped onions and fry till it turns light brown.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes, potatoes and green peas.
  5. Add salt, turmeric powder, cumin powder, and red chilli powder according to your taste.
  6. Cook for five minutes over low heat. Add water according to the amount of dough and vegetables. Let it boil.
  7. In the meantime, we are going to make the skyu dough. It's a simple procedure, take a small chunk of dough, a size of a small marble, and press it with your thumb to make a small circular bowl shape.
  8. When the vegetable stew boils, add the dough. Do not stir and let it boil, until the stew thickens.
  9. Serve it hot in a bowl with a sprinkle of fresh coriander.
