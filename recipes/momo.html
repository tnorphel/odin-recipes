Momo

Description

I am sure many of you have already heard about Momo. It's a pretty famous dish served in many restaurants. Momos are basically steamed or fried dumplings which are stuffed with either vegetables or meat.

Ingredients

  • Refined flour.
  • Green cabbages
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Spinach
  • Cheese curd (optional)
  • Unsalted butter (optional)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper, cumin powder, coriander powder
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Knead the dough. No need to add anything before kneading. Set it aside.
  2. Finely mince all the vegetables: cabbage, carrots, onions, and spinach.
  3. In a deep pan, heat a few teaspoons of cooking oil over low to medium heat.
  4. Add the mixture of minced vegetables, cheese curd, and 2 or 3 small cubes of unsalted butter.
  5. Add salt and the spices.
  6. Cook over low heat until semi-cooked.
  7. Roll the dough over a flat surface. Take a steel glass (a circular object with sharp edge will also do), and cut circular pieces.
  8. Add a spoon of the mixed vegetable and wrap it. Please watch a video on ways to wrap.
  9. Cook the momos in a steamer.
  10. Serve with a home-made chutney, pickle, or tomato ketchup.

If you want to try non-vegetarian, replace the vegetables(excluding onions) with finely minced mutton/chicken/pork/beef. Also there is no need to cook the meat onion mixture. Also, no need to add cheese curd and butter. Rest of the procedures are same.
